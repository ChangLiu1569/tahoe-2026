Paddle smart划桨小贴士

Go out in the morning before the wind. Life jacket on, leash clipped — the water is ~63°F, so a fall is a real shock. Reapply sunscreen; the glare doubles the burn.趁上午起风前下水。穿好救生衣、扣上脚绳——水温约 63°F（17°C），落水会很激。记得补防晒，水面反光会让晒伤加倍。

Crowd plan人潮对策

It's the 4th on a Saturday — big crowds. Claim a spot by early evening, bring layers (~45°F nights), a blanket, and headlamps. Walk from base if you can; budget ~45+ min to get out of parking after.独立日恰逢周六——人很多。傍晚就去占位，带上保暖衣物（夜里约 45°F / 7°C）、毯子和头灯。能走就从大本营走过去；散场后停车场至少要 45 分钟才能出来。
