Paddle smart

Go out in the morning before the wind. Life jacket on, leash clipped — the water is ~63°F, so a fall is a real shock. Reapply sunscreen; the glare doubles the burn.

Beaches

🏖️ Swim & paddle

  • Pope Beach — long sand, pines, grills. (~10 min)
  • Baldwin Beach — easy access, Mt. Tallac views. (~12 min)
  • El Dorado / Lakeview Commons — in-town, rentals, prime fireworks. (~6 min)
  • Nevada Beach — wide NV-side beach, sunset views. (~15 min)
Walks & views

🥾 Easy legs

  • Emerald Bay Overlook — one of CA's most-photographed views. (~20 min)
  • Vikingsholm Trail — ~1 mi down to the bay & castle.
  • Pope Estate / Tallac — flat, free, shaded stroll.
  • Lake of the Sky Trail — short, casual lakeside path.

Most beaches charge ~$12 parking · open ~8a–8p

★ Main event

🎆 Lights on the Lake

  • 🕘 Show: ~9:45 PM, choreographed to music
  • 📻 Soundtrack: stream the synced audio free on your phone
  • 🎺 Parade: 9:00 AM from Ski Run Blvd
  • 👀 Free views: El Dorado, Regan Beach, Nevada Beach
Crowd plan

It's the 4th on a Saturday — big crowds. Claim a spot by early evening, bring layers (~45°F nights), a blanket, and headlamps. Walk from base if you can; budget ~45+ min to get out of parking after.
